Dr. Arun Dungarwal

Chief Consultant Orthopedic Surgeon

Dr. Arun Dungarwal is a renowned orthopedic surgeon in Rajasthan, currently serving as the Chief Consultant at Bone and Joint Multispecialty Hospital, Bhilwara. Specializing in knee and hip surgeries, he performs procedures ranging from minimally invasive arthroscopies to total joint replacements. He completed his post-graduation at **Sawai Man Singh Medical College, Jaipur**, home to one of India's largest orthopedic departments. With specialized training from globally recognized centers, Dr. Dungarwal focuses on knee replacements, arthroscopies, and complex trauma surgeries. Performing over **700+ complex joint surgeries annually**, he is a trusted name in orthopedic care, especially among high-profile athletes.
